Ingredients:
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups grated, peeled raw zucchini
- 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon double-acting baking powder
- 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9 x 5 x 3 inch loaf pans.

 2. Beat the eggs until light and foamy. Add the sugar, oil, zucchini, and vanilla and mix lightly but well. Combine the flour, salt, soda, baking powder, and cinnamon and add to the egg-zucchini mixture. Stir until well blended and add nuts.

 3. Pour into loaf pans. Bake 1 hour. Cool or rack.
